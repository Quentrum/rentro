Why do we know that you'll love using Rentro?

Because you'll be:

  • Making Money

    By renting out your belongings, you get to make money off of your old or unused devices, textbooks, or other school and college life supplies. Why lend out instead of sell? The ability to rent out multiple times at a lower price in comparison to selling your item at a lower price and only making money once.

  • Saving Money

    By renting out products on Rentro, you know that you'll be getting products from other students -not companies- that know and understand what you need, and what it's like spending money on a college student's budget. Why rent out instead of buy? Think of the lab sciences. Why spend hundreds of dollars on textbooks/lab gear/manuals that you'll only be able to use for a year, when you could rent them out for a much lower price?

  • Helping the Environment

    Mass consumption is a huge part of America's carbon footprint. The environmental costs in transporting goods over long distances adds up to being one of the biggest out of all types of pollution. Through Rentro's peer to peer sharing platform, you can focus on deliveries within your community, shortening distances that your products travel, and helping the environment along the way.

  • Helping out other Students

    This is a platform by students, for students. College is expensive, and at the turn of every quarter or semester there are more and more opportunities to take money out of your wallet. By lending on Rentro, you can make some money because you know your product will be going to a body of needing students. By renting out on Rentro you can find what you need at a low price, and help fellow students while at it.

It's easy and safe to use

As a lender all you need to do is:

  • Register

    If you haven't already, create an account. Then you will simply register your item. Don't worry, there will be steps on how to do it to best ensure the safety of your device and the satisfaction of the customer who uses it. Registration should take about 15 minutes.

  • Ship

    Once you get a match for your item, you will ship it to the waiting customer. You will receive all the information you need to do so. Please be sure to include everything needed in the package, and also be sure to package properly. Once that is complete, you can pretty much call it done.

  • Wait

    The easy but hard part. This is what defines Rentro for you, the seller. The longer your item stays on the market, the more chances we have to get it out to waiting customers -- and, the more you are able to earn.

And as a renter all you need to do is:

  • Rent

    If you haven't already, create an account. Next, browse our collection of items that have been put up for rent, and choose what it is you would like to rent out. There will be more directions and information when you are ready to purchase.

  • Enjoy

    The fun part. Responsibly use the item that you purchased for the amount of time you agreed to during purchase. From that time of purchase to the time you return the item, you're legally responsible for it -so make the most of your time.

  • Return

    Package and ship back the item once you are done using it. Please be sure to remember to include everything that came with the item, and also be sure to package appropriately (e.g, using "Fragile" when needed.)

It's safe because:

  • Users are Verified

    We safely store verified information on all Rentro users. This is to verify everyone's identity and so that you know who it is you are in business with.

  • We Review Posts

    We take the time to go through each post that is on our site. We want to ensure that posts follow our guidelines, and keep everyone safe.

  • Help From The Community

    We encourage users on Rentro to report posts that are not following guidelines. This is to add to the amount of safety on our site.
