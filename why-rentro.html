Why do we know that you'll love using Rentro?

Because you'll be:

  • Making Money

    By renting out your belongings, you get to make money off of your old or unused devices, textbooks, or other school and college life supplies. Why lend out instead of sell? The ability to rent out multiple times at a lower price in comparison to selling your item at a lower price and only making money once.

  • Saving Money

    By renting out products on Rentro, you know that you'll be getting products from other students -not companies- that know and understand what you need, and what it's like spending money on a college student's budget. Why rent out instead of buy? Think of the lab sciences. Why spend hundreds of dollars on textbooks/lab gear/manuals that you'll only be able to use for a year, when you could rent them out for a much lower price?

  • Helping the Environment

    Mass consumption is a huge part of America's carbon footprint. The environmental costs in transporting goods over long distances adds up to being one of the biggest out of all types of pollution. Through Rentro's peer to peer sharing platform, you can focus on deliveries within your community, shortening distances that your products travel, and helping the environment along the way.

  • Helping out other Students

    This is a platform by students, for students. College is expensive, and at the turn of every quarter or semester there are more and more opportunities to take money out of your wallet. By lending on Rentro, you can make some money because you know your product will be going to a body of needing students. By renting out on Rentro you can find what you need at a low price, and help fellow students while at it.
