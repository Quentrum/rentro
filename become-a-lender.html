Becoming a Lender on Rentro

Open and free to everyone.

How do I start?

  • Make and Verify your account

    You can do that here.

  • Post an item up for Rent

    You need a picture, and the official name of your product.

  • Keep up on the Details

    Read our terms and the Lender FAQ.

Are my items safe?

Rentro Ensures:
- That all users are verified before being able to rent out a device
- A steady flow of sent and received confirmations between customers

We have included Lemonade's Renter Insurance on this platform, so that you can choose to insure your item for extra assurance.

Someone has rented out my item. Now what?

  • Check Product Description

    Make sure that before you lend out your product, make sure that you include in the product description everything that you are lending out. This includes chargers, cases, etc.

  • Lend out Your Device

    Arrange a pickup date with your customer, or if needed, package your device in accordance to the shipping guidelines and ship your product.
    Read now on Rentro's alternative vision to eliminate the need to deliver on your own /arrange pickups in the future.

  • Confirm the Order

    It is recommended that you submit the lended out confirmation after you do so. It's not mandatory but could be useful if ever needed.
